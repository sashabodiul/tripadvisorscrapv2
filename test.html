Delicious Seasoned Juicy Chicken
Oct 2023 • family
We came in as they opened for lunch at 11am. We got a whole chicken that came with 2 large sides and a dark meat quarter plate that came with 2 small sides. We opted for yucca fries, mashed potatoes, cilantro rice, chickpeas, and added an order of slaw and chicken fried rice. The chicken was juicy, seasoned perfectly with a crispy roasted skin. The yucca fries were golden brown and crisp. The mashed potatoes were real and freshly mashed, cilantro rice was moist and very cilantro-ee. The slaw was creamy and sweet and the chickpeas was seasoned well. The chicken fried rice was surprisingly good. The chicken comes with a green cilantro based spicy sauce (my favorite) and a creamy garlic sauce. Yucca fries with cilantro sauce was the winning side dish and the chicken was hands down best we've had in a long while. Only gripe is we wished that there was some kind of gravy for the mashed potatoes. We definitely will be back.
